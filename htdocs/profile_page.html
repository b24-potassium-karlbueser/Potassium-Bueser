TWICE is an International Girl Group, based in South Korea, under the management of JYP Entertainment. The K-Pop Girl Group is composed of 9 members, namely, Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu.

JYP Entertainment formed the Girl Group through the survival show, Sixteen, where the girls competed with each other to debut in "TWICE." Fortunately, the girls successfully debuted in TWICE on October 20, 2015, with their hit song "Like Ooh-Ahh."

And now, they are dominating the whole world as they spread their "color-pop" sound, which varies from cute pop to contemporary concepts, with their hit songs like "I Can't Stop Me", "Fancy", "What is Love?", and many more! They are finally known in the whole world as the "Queens of K-Pop" and the girl group whose bond is unbreakable. Now that you know TWICE, enjoy listening to their songs and feel special!
